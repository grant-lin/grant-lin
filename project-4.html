We were inspired by Rhianna's beauty makeup line Fenty and decided to be a low budget Fahionista for a day.

Fenty Beauty is a cosmetic, makeup line created by Rhianna. Both being a fan of Rhianna, Tina Do Tran and I teamed up for a light hearted shoot for fun over a Sunday.

Background

Centering around the color red, we want to make something that’s got a little bit of attitude, sass, passion but not sexual. At the same time, we also just want to make cool images.

Prop shopping

I used Micheals props to really enhance the shot, and did a shopping spree at target, thrifted some clothing to put together the shots you see below. (along with a lot of post editing)
